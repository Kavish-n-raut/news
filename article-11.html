Domestic Deal Wins vs Global Softness

IT stocks saw mixed movement as domestic deal wins clashed with signals of global softness. Infosys and TCS announced major digital transformation mandates from public-sector clients, boosting confidence in their India pipeline. But sentiment cooled when industry trackers noted slower hiring and reduced bench deployment across the broader IT space, hinting that companies remain cautious about long-term demand.

The session remained choppy as traders tried to balance optimism from large local contracts against concerns about weaker discretionary spending in Western markets. A few brokers noted that deal closures abroad are taking longer than expected, especially in BFSI and telecom verticals.

The Nifty IT index ended almost unchanged, with selective buying offset by selling in stocks perceived to be more exposed to margin pressure. Analysts said domestic deals are helpful but may not fully offset global hesitations unless conversion cycles improve.
